This session is for HTML lists

There are two types of lists

  • Ordered lists
  • Un-ordered lists

Un-ordered lists

Un-ordered lists can use markers such as square, circle and discs

Below are examples

  • Brazil
  • Italy
  • Germany
  • Argentia
  • Brazil
  • Italy
  • Germany
  • Argentia
  • Brazil
  • Italy
  • Argentia
  • Home
  • News
  • Contact
  • About
